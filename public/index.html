Kingswood Village, Kings Beach, California

Located just five miles southeaset of Northstar Ski Resort, and centrally located to eleven other ski resorts in the Tahoe area, our condo is just under a mile from Lake Tahoe’s north shore. Kingswood Village offers a large pool and wading pool built in 2001 as well as three tennis courts.

Roomy yet homey, our three-level condo is fully stocked with amenities including washer, dryer, dishwasher, new ceramic cook top and oven, microwave, coffee maker, gas grill, indoor and outdoor dining options, and much more.
